The game Star Wars Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy, was a huge part of my childhood and contained one of my favourite modifications made for a game, Makermod.

The modification was originally created by a mod author called Toast and was eventually succeeded by Scooper.

Makermod essentially allowed you to create anything, from small duelling platforms to majestic temples and devastating battle stations.

This page will contain a few plugins for Makermod that I've kept around and would love to share with like-minded players so that they are never lost to time
